## Laither

**Laither** is the personal studio of Lei Wang, registered in Nanjing (Pukou District), 2026.

The name echoes the idea that every second stacks on the one before it — small bits of automation and tooling, compounded over time, is what the studio is about.

## What I build

- **Web / SaaS**: `stock.laither.com` (investment review) · `math.laither.com` (K-12 math & English contest prep)
- **iOS apps** (Phase 3, late 2026): Flutter cross-platform + SwiftUI native
- **Open-source tools**: Android habit-reminder app · cross-device file archiver · iPhone photo scanner

## About me

Lei Wang, full-stack engineer. Day-to-day Java / C++ / Kotlin / Python. Day job: phone-to-car connectivity (screen casting, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi subsystems) at a phone OEM. Nights and weekends: indie products.
