### stock_quant · Investment review tool

A stock-picking and portfolio-review system for individual investors.

- Status: Phase 1, subscription launch planned for June 2026
- Stack: Python + Streamlit + SQLite + akshare
- Key features: daily 4-dimension review over a 190-ticker watchlist · 3-tier industry lifecycle model · market-wide volume-breakout board
- Pricing: ¥38/month (roughly $5)
- URL: `stock.laither.com` (live after launch)

See the [landing page](/en/works/stock-quant/) for details.
